ClauseInsight
Review agreements with confidence.
ClauseInsight is an intelligent agreement review workspace designed for startups and small businesses. It helps teams analyze agreements faster by automatically identifying missing clauses, assessing compliance, detecting risks, and generating actionable recommendations before contracts are approved or signed.

Built for the Gappy AI – Ship to Get Hired Hackathon 2026, powered by Lemma SDK.
Problem Statement
Businesses review contracts and agreements manually, making the process slow, inconsistent, and prone to oversight. Critical provisions such as termination rights, liability limitations, payment obligations, data protection requirements, and dispute resolution mechanisms are often overlooked, increasing legal, financial, and operational risk. ClauseInsight streamlines agreement review by automatically classifying documents, identifying missing clauses, assessing compliance, detecting risks, and generating actionable recommendations before agreements are approved or signed.
